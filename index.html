About Us

Our Products

Epiphone (GIBSON)

Epiphone is still used as a brand for the Gibson company, both for budget models of other Gibson-branded products and for several Epiphone-exclusive models.

Yamaha FG Series

The FG/FGX Series has louder and stronger sound in the low-to-mid ranges thanks to cutting-edge acoustic analysis technology developed by the Yamaha R&D Division.

Gretsch

Gretsch guitars are incredibly versatile and are especially well-suited for genres like rockabilly, blues, jazz, indie, alternative, country, and Americana.

Godin

Guitars under the Godin brand are primarily mid-range to top-of the-line electrics that are made of high quality wood from the northeastern part of North America.

Fender

Fender produces acoustic guitars, bass amplifiers and public address equipment; however, it is best known for its solid-body electric guitars and bass guitars, particularly the Stratocaster, Telecaster, Jaguar, Jazzmaster, Precision Bass, and the Jazz Bass.

Taylor

An exciting collection of beautifully appointed limited-edition guitars commemorates five decades of craftsmanship, innovation and dedication to players. An all-new acoustic and vocal amplifier blends signature Taylor woodworking, stylish aesthetics, rich sound and broad utility into one essential musical tool.
